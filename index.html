Our Heritage

Over 30 Years of Technical Excellence

On April 18th, 1994, Craw Hammer Trades School opened its doors with a single mission: to provide Zambian youths with the practical tools to build their own future.

What started as a small welding shed has grown into a premier TEVETA-accredited institution. We have spent three decades refining our curriculum to match the evolving needs of the Copperbelt's industrial and mining sectors.

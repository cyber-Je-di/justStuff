Metal Fabrication & Welding

Essential training for Zambia's industrial sector. Our students master specialized joining techniques, structural fabrication, and industry-standard safety protocols.

Key Learning: Blueprint reading, oxy-acetylene cutting, MIG/TIG welding, plate and structural fabrication, and industrial safety practices. Assessment combines practical trade tests and short theory modules.

Career Outcomes: Structural fabricator, weld technician, workshop supervisor, or self-employed fabricator. Employers include mining contractors, construction firms and local fabrication shops.

Trade Test Certificate (Level I, II, III)

Duration: 3-12 Months | Entry: Primary/Grade 7 or 9 Certificate

Craft Certificate

Duration: 2 Years | Entry: Grade 12 Certificate

Advanced Certificate (Technician)

Duration: 2 Years 8 Months | Entry: 5 Credits (incl. Maths, English, Science)

Electrical Technology

Focuses on electrical systems, wiring, and industrial power maintenance. Graduates are equipped for both residential and commercial electrical works.

Key Learning: Electrical safety, domestic and three-phase wiring, motor controls, basic PLC concepts and fault-finding techniques. Training includes hands-on labs and simulated fault exercises.

Career Outcomes: Residential/commercial electrician, maintenance technician, or site electrical supervisor. Graduates are prepared for installation, maintenance and commissioning roles.

Trade Test Level I, II, III

Duration: 1 Year total | Entry: Grade 7 or 9 Certificate

Craft Certificate

Duration: 2 Years | Entry: Grade 12 Certificate
